# Dataminr (@Dataminr)
Dataminr is a leading real-time information discovery platform that uses AI to detect high-impact events and emerging risks from publicly available data sources. Their Twitter account shares alerts and insights on breaking news, crisis events, natural disasters, security incidents, and major developments globally. Known for early detection capabilities often ahead of traditional media. High reliability due to algorithmic verification and professional curation, though commercial platform nature means focus on demonstrable, high-impact events.
