# Nasa (@Nasa)
Official Twitter account for the National Aeronautics and Space Administration. Provides announcements on space missions, satellite operations, Earth observation data, space weather events, International Space Station activities, and aerospace technology developments. Critical source for monitoring space-based intelligence capabilities, orbital activities, satellite imagery for verification, and space domain awareness relevant to security applications.
