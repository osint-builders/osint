# TankerTrackers (@TankerTrackers)
TankerTrackers monitors global oil and gas tanker movements, providing real-time intelligence on energy shipments, strategic reserves, and geopolitical implications. Tracks vessel positions, cargo types, routes, and sanctions evasion. Highly reliable source for energy sector OSINT.
