# US Pacific Fleet (@USPacificFleet)
Official Twitter account of US Pacific Fleet, the largest numbered fleet command in the Navy operating across the Pacific and Indian Oceans. Provides strategic-level announcements on fleet operations, deployments, major exercises, and theater security cooperation from the West Coast to the Indian Ocean. Complements 7th Fleet tactical updates with broader strategic context on Indo-Pacific maritime operations, China deterrence, and alliance coordination.
