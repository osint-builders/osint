# US Navy (@USNavy)
Official Twitter account of the United States Navy, serving as the primary communications channel for the Department of the Navy. Provides authoritative announcements on fleet operations, deployments, ship movements, naval exercises, strategic initiatives, and global maritime security operations. Essential source for monitoring US naval power projection, carrier strike group movements, and maritime domain awareness across all theaters.
